Waitlist privacy notice

This notice covers the one thing {{ config.brand_legal_name }} collects on this site: the email address you give the launch waitlist. {{ config.brand_legal_name }} (Estonia) is the data controller.

What is collected

Your email address, plus the date and time you submitted it.

Why, and the lawful basis

Solely to send you a single email when mentoring launches. The lawful basis is your consent, given via the checkbox on the form. You can withdraw it at any time.

Who processes it

The form is handled by Formspree, which stores submissions on {{ config.brand_legal_name }}'s behalf as a data processor. No data is sold or used for advertising.

How long it is kept

Until launch emails have been sent, or until you ask for removal — whichever is sooner. After that the waitlist is deleted.

Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or erase your data, or withdraw consent, by emailing {{ config.email }}. You may also complain to the Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate (AKI).
